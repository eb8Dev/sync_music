# Privacy Policy - Sync Music

**Last Updated: January 25, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Sync Music ("the App," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Sync Music application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Username/Display Name:** When you use the App, you may provide a username or display name to be shown to other party members.
- **Support Tickets:** When you use the in-app support feature, we collect your subject, message, device platform version, and category of the request to assist you.
- **Email Address:** If you contact us via email for support, we collect the email address you provide.

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we automatically collect the following information:

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers (UUID), mobile network information.
- **Persistent User ID:** We generate a random Unique Identifier (UUID) stored locally on your device to maintain your identity as a "Host" across sessions and server restarts. This ID is not linked to any personal data unless you voluntarily provide it in support tickets.
- **App Usage Data:** Features used, party creation/joining activity, interaction patterns.
- **Analytics Data:** How you interact with the App, performance metrics, crash logs.
- **Location Information:** General location data may be inferred from network information (not used for specific tracking).
- **Push Notification Data:** Notification preferences, delivery status, and engagement metrics.

### 2.3 Information from Third-Party Services

- **Firebase Analytics:** We use Google Firebase to collect analytics data about app usage.
- **Firebase Crashlytics:** We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and error logs.
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM):** We use Firebase Cloud Messaging for push notifications.
- **YouTube Data API:** When you search for and play music, the App interacts with YouTube API services. By using this feature, you agree to be bound by the Google Privacy Policy (http://www.google.com/policies/privacy).

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Service Provision:** To enable core functionality including party creation, synchronization, real-time features, and persistent host identity.
- **Support System:** To process and respond to your support tickets submitted through the App.
- **Notifications:** To send push notifications about party events, app updates, and important announcements.
- **Analytics and Improvement:** To analyze app usage, identify issues, and improve the App's features and performance.
- **Error Reporting:** To diagnose and fix technical issues and crashes.
- **Compliance:** To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

## 4. How We Share Your Information

### 4.1 Information Shared With Other Users

When you participate in a party on Sync Music:
- Your username/display name is visible to other party members.
- Other users can see your interactions (reactions, queue additions).
- The host can see that you are connected to the party.

### 4.2 Information Shared With Third Parties

- **Firebase Services:** We share analytics, crash reports, and performance data with Google Firebase.
- **YouTube:** When you search for or play music, YouTube collects data as per their policies.
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

### 4.3 Information We Do NOT Share

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 5. Data Retention

- **User Identity (UUID):** Stored locally on your device until you clear app data or uninstall.
- **Support Tickets:** Retained until the issue is resolved or for a period necessary for service improvement.
- **Analytics Data:** Retained for reporting and analysis purposes and may be deleted after a reasonable period.
- **Party Data:** Information about specific parties is typically retained only during the party session and short grace periods for reconnection.

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.
