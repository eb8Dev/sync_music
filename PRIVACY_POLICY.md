# Privacy Policy - Sync Music

**Last Updated: January 23, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Sync Music ("the App," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Sync Music application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Username/Display Name:** When you use the App, you may provide a username or display name to be shown to other party members
- **Email Address:** If you contact us for support, we collect the email address you provide

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we automatically collect the following information:

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
- **App Usage Data:** Features used, party creation/joining activity, interaction patterns
- **Analytics Data:** How you interact with the App, performance metrics, crash logs
- **Location Information:** General location data may be inferred from network information (not used for tracking)
- **Push Notification Data:** Notification preferences, delivery status, and engagement metrics (whether notifications were opened/clicked)

### 2.3 Information from Third-Party Services

- **Firebase Analytics:** We use Google Firebase to collect analytics data about app usage
- **Firebase Crashlytics:** We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and error logs
- **Firebase Remote Config:** We use remote configuration for app feature management
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM):** We use Firebase Cloud Messaging for push notifications
- **YouTube Data:** When you search for and play music, YouTube may collect related data through the YouTube Player

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Service Provision:** To enable core functionality including party creation, synchronization, real-time features, and push notifications
- **Notifications:** To send push notifications about party events, app updates, campaigns, and important announcements
- **Analytics and Improvement:** To analyze app usage, identify issues, and improve the App's features and performance
- **Error Reporting:** To diagnose and fix technical issues and crashes
- **Support:** To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
- **Compliance:** To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service
- **Communication:** To send you updates about the App, security alerts, or important notices

## 4. How We Share Your Information

### 4.1 Information Shared With Other Users

When you participate in a party on Sync Music:

- Your username/display name is visible to other party members
- Other users can see your interactions (reactions, queue additions)
- The host can see that you are connected to the party

This information is only shared within the context of the specific party you're participating in.

### 4.2 Information Shared With Third Parties

- **Firebase Cloud Messaging:** We use Firebase Cloud Messaging to send push notifications to your device
- **Firebase Services:** We share analytics, crash report, and performance data with Google Firebase for service improvement
- **YouTube:** When you search for or play music, YouTube collects data as per their policies
- **Service Providers:** We may share information with service providers who assist in operating the App
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety

### 4.3 Information We Do NOT Share

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We do not share your information with advertisers without your explicit consent.
